# SmartCities
Building Cities for the Future 

how AI and technology are reshaping four cities that represent four different urban worlds — Bangalore, New York, Newark, and Dubai — tracing each city's trajectory from its early smart-city experiments to its current reality to its committed future plans. Across all four, the same pattern emerges: grand platform-scale "smart city" visions largely failed, while narrow AI tools solving specific problems — permits, construction, energy, inspections — are quietly succeeding, though each city's progress is shaped by its own governance, money, and climate. The comparison reveals both universal yet to be solved and city-specific ones, pointing to where the next generation of urban AI will be built.
